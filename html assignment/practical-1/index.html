About Pubg

PUBG: Battlegrounds (previously known as Player Unknown's Battlegrounds) is a 2017 battle royale game developed by PUBG Studios and published by Krafton.The game,which was inspired by 2000's japanese film battle royale, is based on previous mods created by brendan "Player Unknown" Greene for other games, & expanded into a standalone game.

Gameplay

PUBG is a player versus player shooter game in which up to one hundred players fight in a battle royale, a type of large-scale last man standing deathmatch where players fight to remain the last alive.

Development

The game's concept and design was led by Brendan Greene, better known by his online handle PlayerUnknown

Design

PUBG represents the standalone version of what Greene believes is the "final version" of the battle royale concept, incorporating the elements he had designed.
