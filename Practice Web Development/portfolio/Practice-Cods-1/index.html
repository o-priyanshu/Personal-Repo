My Introduction

Hello, I'm Priyanshu Thapa. I am currently studying at Dev Bhoomi University, enrolled in the BSc IT program, with the batch number 2024-2027. As a beginner or fresher in the field, I am actively working on developing my skills. I reside in Dehradun, in the village of Selaquie, Telpura, with my parents and one younger brother. To be honest, I don't have a strong, singular inspiration or aspiration like some others who may have more defined goals. I am pursuing coding out of curiosity and interest, with a personal plan to potentially start my own company in the future. While I am still exploring what type of services I might offer, I hope to define this more clearly as I gain experience and if everything aligns well. Though it may sound like a distant dream, I believe it will depend on my hard work and dedication to my chosen profession. That said, I am not opposed to working with established companies. I would greatly value the opportunity to work in an environment that offers growth, experience, and a supportive atmosphere, along with a fair salary, which I believe is important for motivation and morale. I would be enthusiastic about being part of a company that genuinely cares for its employees.
